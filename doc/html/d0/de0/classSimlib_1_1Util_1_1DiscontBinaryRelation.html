Gets the projection of elements to their representatives.

This method relies on the fact that *this represents an equivalance relation (the result is undefined otherwise). The method creates the projection of elements to their representatives in the quotient set induced by the equivalence relation represented by *this.

Parameters
[out]quot_projThe projection of elements to their representatives in the quotient set
